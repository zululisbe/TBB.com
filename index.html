A little bit about the bottled bushbaby.

Started in 2016, our little workshop has been making unique and one of a kind glassware from throw away bottles, each item is cut, sanded, polished and ecthed by hand. All our bottles come from various resturants and bars ( and the occasional house party!)

Any bottles or shards that are not used are collected and dropped at a glass factory.We don't have a shop (yet) so please check the socal media links to find out which craft fair we will be at next or feel free to contact us direct for any information.
